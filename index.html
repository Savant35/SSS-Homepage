  • Services
  • Eligibility
  • About Trio

TRIO Student Support Services (SSS/TRIO) is a federally-funded program that motivates and empowers students from under-represented populations in their pursuit of educational, career, and life goals. Students have access to one to one advising and mentoring to assist them in managing academic, financial, and social transitions during their time in college. The program is dedicated to increasing the retention and graduation rates for first-generation college students, from income-eligible households, and/or students with a documented disability.
